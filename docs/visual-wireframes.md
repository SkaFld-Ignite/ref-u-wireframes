# 📱 Ref-U Platform: Visual Wireframes & Mockups

*Complete visual design specifications for client presentation and investor showcase*

## 🎯 Executive Summary

The **Ref-U Platform** revolutionizes sports fan engagement through real-time referee analysis, educational content, and community building. These visual wireframes demonstrate the complete user experience from onboarding to monetization.

### Key Features Showcased
- 🏀 **Real-time voting** on referee calls during live games
- 📚 **Educational content** explaining complex sports rules
- 👥 **Community cohorts** for team-based discussions
- 🎮 **Gamification system** with XP, levels, and achievements
- 💰 **Multiple revenue streams** through premium and commerce
